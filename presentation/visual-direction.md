# Visual Direction

This is a simple presentation wrapper for a business-cohort submission.

## Visual Premise

Show contrast:

- messy product hype before the operator;
- clean decision path inside the folder;
- one business action after the operator.

## Color Roles

Use restrained, high-contrast colors with one accent per decision state:

| Role | Suggested color | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Base | charcoal / near-black | headers, folder structure, serious business tone |
| Paper | white / light gray | screenshots, rule cards, output blocks |
| PROMOTE | green | cleared opportunity |
| SKIP | red | disqualified or structurally weak product |
| WATCHLIST | amber | promising but not proven |

Avoid making the whole presentation neon, playful, or overly TikTok-branded. The submission is about business discipline applied to a creator workflow.

## Slide Or Screen Sequence

1. **Title:** TikTok Shop Product Scout
   - Subtitle: Governed product selection for affiliate creators
   - Cohort: Business

2. **The Problem:** Viral product signals are noisy
   - Price, hype, commission, creator saturation, claims, and audience fit all compete for attention.

3. **The Folder:** Judgment becomes a workflow
   - Show `identity.md`, `rules.md`, `reference/`, and output templates.

4. **The Path:** Disqualify, score, threshold, edge case, output
   - Use the decision-path visual from `presentation/README.md`.

5. **The Demo:** Touchscreen phone wallet
   - Show product input on the left and the WATCHLIST output on the right.

6. **The Business Value:** Protect time, trust, and monetization
   - Close on the fact that a creator leaves with one action.

## Caption Style

Use short captions that name the system behavior:

- "Compliance checks happen before scoring."
- "Missing commission becomes a watchlist condition."
- "The output is a business action, not advice."
- "Swap the audience profile; keep the decision path."
