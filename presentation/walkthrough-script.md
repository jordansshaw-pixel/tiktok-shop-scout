# Walkthrough Script

Target length: 60-90 seconds.

Use this for a captioned video, Loom-style walkthrough, or live demo narration.

## Hook

Most AI tools answer a creator's question. This folder governs the decision.

TikTok Shop affiliates do not just need more product ideas. They need to know which products deserve filming time, audience trust, and compliance risk.

## Problem

A creator sees a product going viral and asks, "Should I promote this?"

The normal AI answer is soft: maybe, it depends, check the reviews, think about your audience.

That is not enough when the creator has to film today.

## Solution

TikTok Shop Product Scout is a folder-based AI operator. A product listing comes in, and one decision comes out:

- **PROMOTE**
- **SKIP**
- **WATCHLIST**

The folder forces the model through a business workflow:

1. Check hard disqualifiers first.
2. Score the product across six business dimensions.
3. Apply thresholds.
4. Handle edge cases.
5. Ship the required output format.

## Demo Setup

For example, here is a touchscreen crossbody phone wallet from TikTok Shop.

It has a good price point, clear demo potential, and visible buyer appeal. But the commission is unknown.

The operator does not guess its way into a promotion.

## Demo Result

Because commission is unconfirmed, the correct decision is **WATCHLIST**.

The output names the exact flip condition: confirm commission at 8 percent or higher within seven days.

If that proof appears, promote it with the saved hook. If not, move it to the rejection log.

## Close

That is the business value.

The folder turns product scouting from creator instinct into a repeatable decision system. It protects time, trust, and monetization before content work starts.

The TikTok Shop use case is the demo. The underlying pattern is governed AI judgment.
